About Me

"I am a teacher of mathematics and information technology at a college, and since early 2024, I have been actively developing as a web developer. I started my journey with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and then began exploring full-stack development. Initially, I worked with React, but after exploring Vue, I found it more well-thought-out and more to my liking, so I switched to it. My current project is being developed using Vue 3, along with TypeScript, Pinia, and SCSS on the frontend. On the backend, I work with Hono, Prisma, and PostgreSQL. Additionally, I handle authentication (JWT, Google OAuth), S3 storage, automated testing (Vitest, Playwright, Cypress), and deployment with Docker."

Nat's Doll

A full-stack B2C online store for handcrafted polymer-clay dolls. The project covers the complete shopping flow — gallery, product pages, registration and login (including Google OAuth), cart, and PayPal checkout — backed by a typed API, a PostgreSQL database, and image storage in S3. The frontend follows Feature-Sliced Design, the codebase is fully typed end-to-end, and the application is deployed to a VPS with Docker Compose.

Technologies used: Vue 3, Pinia, TypeScript, Vite, SCSS, Hono, Node.js, Prisma, PostgreSQL, JWT, Google OAuth, PayPal, Yandex Object Storage (S3), Vitest, Playwright, Docker
